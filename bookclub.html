BOOK CLUB

AIM:

To include the precious habit reading in students...

OBJECTIVES

  • To help students understand the importance of reading in general.
  • To discuss the vital in selected work of literature.
  • To expose some old works of literature and discuss the importance.

Activities

  • Book explanation in 3m
    • In this activity they explain the author,highlightsand main core of the book.
  • Debate on book
    • In this activity he/she discusses about the plot of the book and in general about various points the book highlights.
