CLUB SPARK

Aim:

To gather videos with help of students in order to promote technical knowledge ..

Objectives:

  • To provide visuals that can help students learn new technologies
  • To provide the students with a chance to amass loads of technical skill in brief span

Activities

  • Technical Videos
    • To expose students to latest technological trends and visualize workings.
  • Video Editing
    • Time limits are set for each video to sharpen editing skills. This activity helps students improve in multiple disciplines and saves time.
