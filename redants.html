FINE ARTS

AIM:

The club is a student run fine arts club which primarly aims at honing the finer skills that are vested in the students.

OBJECTIVES

  • To expose the students of the college to situations similar to the one they will be changed with the real world.
  • To unite the students of the college under the culture fold.
  • TO encourage the students to utilize their creativity & enhance the hidden skills.

Activities

  • Pencil Shading.
  • T-Shirt Painting.
  • Face Painting.
  • Mehandhi design.
  • Rangoli
